Affordability in Auckland

You just got a job in Auckland. Now where to live?
This application color codes Auckland's neighborhoods by their affordability to you based on rent, commute, car ownership, and parking costs determined by your answers to the questions below.

About

  • This application was created by Alex Raichev and Abhishek Reddy and was inspired in part by the paper "Housing and transport expenditure: Socio-spatial indicators of affordability in Auckland" by K. Mattingly and J. Morrisseyc [MaMo2014].
  • The neighborhoods in the map are Auckland's 2013 census area units sourced from Statistics New Zealand here.
  • Rent per bedroom per area unit comes from the New Zealand Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment from the detailed mean rents file here. Since the suburbs of the rent data don't match the census area units (see this thread for details), we mapped the area units to suburbs in a many-to-one fashion as detailed in this CSV file.
  • The median annual income of employed Aucklanders is sourced from Statistics New Zealand from the June 2013 income survey here
