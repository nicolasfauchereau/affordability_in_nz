About

  • This application was created by Alex Raichev and is inspired in part by the article "Housing and transport expenditure: Socio-spatial indicators of affordability in Auckland" by K. Mattingly and J. Morrissey [MaMo2014].
  • Auckland's 2013 census area units come from Statistics New Zealand here.
  • The median annual income of employed Aucklanders comes from Statistics New Zealand from the June 2013 income survey here.
  • Mean rent per area unit per number of bedrooms comes from the New Zealand Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment from the detailed mean rents file here. Since the suburbs of the rent data doesn't match the census area units (see this thread for details), the area units are mapped to the suburbs in a many-to-one fashion as detailed in this CSV file. This data will be replaced soon by rent data from the 2013 census.
  • The distances used are those between area unit centroids and were calculated as the bird flies. They'll be recalculated soon through the road network.
  • Commute costs per km come from [MaMo2014, Table 2] and are (walk, bicycle, car, bus) = (0, 0, $0.274, $0.218).
  • Car ownership cost per car per year comes from [MaMo2014, Table 3] and is $2228.
